Sometimes
...

We get trapped in our own heads

In a thought bubble.

Poor mental health
impacts our decision-making
in our day-to-day lives. We may sometimes
feel trapped by an inability to find motivation
while tasks that demand our attention pile up.

Poor mental health happens anyway, whether through circumstance or living with a mental health disorder, but a lack of Dopamine in the brain sometimes makes it difficult to make choices that will move us forward.

This webiste is a Thought Visualizer, allowing you to get your jumbled thoughts into the open, and pick through them one by one.

Check the About section for a guide,
or explore at your own pace.
